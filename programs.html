PROGRAMS OVERVIEW

Early childhood education is one of the best ways to help your child develop
the social, emotional, and cognitive skills required for
primary school and beyond, according to research.


4 Years Old


The programme focuses on primary developmental areas such as language arts, literacy, mathematics, science, social and emotional development, gross and fine motor skill development, and character development.

4-year-olds Basic Skils:
1. Sing a song.
2. Overhand catch and throw a ball.
3. Make a block tower out of 10 blocks.
4. Recognize the distinction between fantasy and reality.
5. Make a circle and a square.
6. Will ask questions constantly.
7. May know one color or more.


5 Years Old


The knowledge and skills that these children gain in preschool is very important for their future as whatever learning and social skills they acquire today are the very basic foundation that will shape your child's future in later years.

5-year-olds Basic Skils:
1. May put six to eight words together to form a sentence.
2. May recognise four or more colours.
3. Understands the weeks and months.
4. Can identify coins and money.
5. Commands with multiple instructions can be understood.
6. Frequently converses.


6 Years Old


The information and abilities that these children acquire in preschool are essential for their years ahead because whatever social and educational capabilities they attain today will form the very basic foundation that will shape your children's future in older years.

6-year-olds Basic Skils:
1. Can copy simple shapes with a pencil.
2. Copy letters and write their own name, as well as their full name, address, age, and birthday.
3. Draw more realistic images, such as a person with a head, eyes, mouth, and nose, and a body with arms and legs.

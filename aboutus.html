Our History

The Green Sprouts International School was established in August of
2010 as a private institution of early learning and development where
young children of all backgrounds, races, and ethnic heritages could
learn and grow together. Mrs. Najatul Syamimi Binti Shamsul Azeze
founded the Green Sprouts International School . Her first school,
Happy Days Preschool was established in 2005 when women rapidly
joined the workforce and needed quality care for their children. With
three students, Mrs. Syamimi vision of affordable, quality preschools
designed for the working family began. The Green Sprouts International
School remains a family owned and operated business with a commitment
to provide a sage, nurturing, environment in a family like atmosphere.

Our Mission & Vision

At The Green Sprouts International School, we believe that early childhood education is essential to the development of well rounded, eager, students. Our mission is to provide an engaging, stimulating, and fun environment in which children of all backgrounds can work together to learn and thrive. We kept our registration fees low, tuition and associated costs affordable because we don't think that anyone should be excluded from a high quality private education.
